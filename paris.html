About Paris, France

Paris is the capital of the country of France. It has long been one of western Europe's major centers of culture and business. Some of the world's greatest artists, writers, scholars, fashion designers, and chefs have lived in Paris. There's a reason Paris is one of the most popular cities among travelers. Known as the “City of Light” or the “City of Love,” the streets of Paris overflow with culture, art, beauty, and history.
