About Dubai, United Arab Emirates

The city of glittering skyscrapers, luxury malls, and sun-soaked beaches. With its mix of glamour, desert adventures, and awe-inspiring landmarks like the Burj Khalifa. Because of its large expatriate population, it feels like a Middle Eastern melting pot, and the atmosphere is generally tolerant. Religious affiliations are not a prominent aspect of city life.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival, Hotel Check-In and city introduction
  • Day 2: must-visit places include the Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest building, the Palm Jumeirah, a man-made island, and the Dubai Mall, a shopper's paradise.
  • Day 3: Trip to Dubai Fountain, the world's largest choreographed fountain system, and the Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo. For outdoor adventures, consider Ski Dubai, an indoor ski resort
  • Day 4: Try some more adventure activities and explore the desert by camel or ATV, and the Dubai Miracle Garden, a stunning floral display.
  • Day5-7: Free exploration
